| Product      | Weekday | Weekend   |
| :---:       |    :----:   |   :---:     |
| Banana      | 2.50       | 2.70 |
| Apple   | 1.30        | 1.60    |
| Kiwi   | 2.20        | 3.00    |

We want to calculate the price for a certain product, depending on the day of the week:
